It's great to find peers who are equally interested in improving and evolving, not just as a programmer but also as a human being. I recently found one.

<!--more-->
A few months ago, I signed up for Martin Jonen's newsletter. Usually, technical content is boring: to achieve X, do Y. But there are exceptions. And those exceptions deserve to be praised and shared.

Instead of focusing on the technologies themselves, Martin focuses on the people who use them and their physical, psychological, and spiritual well-being. His advice has been invaluable, and I recommend anyone interested in SAP and ABAP to subscribe to his newsletter.

Sign up on [his site][1].

Thank you, Martin, and I hope you continue to guide us.

Greetings from Abapinho.
